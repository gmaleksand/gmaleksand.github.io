$$v = v_0; a_n = g\cos\alpha; r_O = \frac{v_0^2}{a_n} = \frac{v_0^2}{g\cos\alpha}.$$

$$v = v_0 \cos\alpha; a_n = g; r_M = \frac{v^2}{a_n} = \frac{v_0^2 \cos^2\alpha}{g}$$
